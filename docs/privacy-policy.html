LiftVault Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

LiftVault is a workout tracking app that stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

All workout data, templates, and settings are stored entirely on your device using Apple's on-device storage. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Data Collection

LiftVault does not collect any personal data. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Third-Party Services

LiftVault does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
